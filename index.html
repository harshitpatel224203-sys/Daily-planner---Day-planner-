Privacy Policy

Plain-language details on what Daily planner - Day planner does and doesn't do with your information.

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Daily planner - Day planner ("the app") is an independent, ad-supported to-do list application for Android. This policy explains what information is, and is not, collected when you use the app.

Information we collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit your task data anywhere. Every task you create, check off, or delete is saved only in your device's local app storage. There is no account, no server, and no database on our end — we have no way to see your task list, even if we wanted to.

Third-party services

The app uses Google AdMob to show banner advertisements, which is how the app stays free to use. As part of showing ads, Google's Mobile Ads SDK may collect information such as:

  • A device advertising identifier
  • General device and app information (e.g. device model, OS version)
  • Approximate location and IP address, used for ad relevance and fraud prevention

This collection is carried out by Google, governed by Google's own privacy policy, available at policies.google.com/privacy. You can review and reset your advertising identifier, or opt out of personalized ads, in your device's Google settings.

Permissions

The app requests internet access solely so that advertisements can load. It does not request access to your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or files, and does not need any of those permissions to function.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a third-party ad service, please contact us so we can look into it.

Data retention and deletion

Because all task data lives only on your device, uninstalling the app or clearing its storage from your device settings permanently deletes it. We hold no separate copy to retain or delete.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time as the app changes. Any changes will be posted on this page along with a new "last updated" date.
